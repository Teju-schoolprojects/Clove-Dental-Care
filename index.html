Frequently Asked Questions

We believe in transparent pricing and clear clinic operations. Have questions? Find quick details below.

What should I bring to my first dental appointment?

+
Please bring a government-issued photo ID, your insurance card (if applicable), and any recent dental radiographs taken within the past 12 months. If you have a complex medical history, please bring a list of your current prescriptions.

Do you accept insurance? How do insurance co-pays work?

+
Yes, we accept major dental insurance networks including Delta Dental, Cigna, Aetna, MetLife, and United Healthcare. Our system files claims directly for you. You are responsible for any co-pays or deductibles at the time of service, which we estimate beforehand.

What is the difference between silver and composite white fillings?

+
Silver amalgam fillings are highly durable metal alloys, whereas composite fillings are tooth-colored resin mixtures. We exclusively utilize premium composite resins at Clove Dental. They match your tooth's natural color perfectly and bond directly to the tooth structure, preserving more healthy tooth material.

How often should I visit the clinic for routine cleanings?

+
For the majority of patients, we recommend scheduling an exam and cleanings every 6 months to prevent plaque build-up and detect decay early. Patients with active periodontal (gum) concerns may benefit from cleanings every 3 to 4 months.

What do I do if I have a dental emergency over the weekend?

+
We maintain an emergency hotline for active dental emergencies (severe pain, bleeding, or knocked-out teeth) on weekends. Call +1 (555) 321-CLOVE and press option 9 to reach our on-call dental surgeon. We offer prioritized emergency slots on Monday mornings.
